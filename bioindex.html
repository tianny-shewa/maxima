Maxima Biography.

Oluwafemi Maxima was born into the family on August 6th 1880. He grew up in Osogbo, Osun state. As a child, maxima grew up as an easy-going but high-spirited child.

In 1890,Maxima moved to Osogbo,where he enrolled in a private school as a primary v student. While he was a secondary school student,he enjoyed playing tasking games with people. Even though he had no specific person as a friend, he was an extrovert who tends to get into everybody's pool of attention. He graduated from the school in 1895 and proceeded into Osun state college of education for his NCE program which he completed in the year 1990...

At age 20, Maxima moved to the city and he started a career out of what he has always loved to do - teaching. He learned to play some instrument within a short period of time, since he's always been a chorister from his childhood days,he found it easy to join any christian singing group and blend in wihin a short period of time.
